Background

Moffat Teya's career, filled with sharp contrasts, is an inspiring story of what a person with a vision can accomplish when obsessed with original idea and afire with enthusiasm. Born and raised in Kipkebe Tea Estate, he hardly wore a pair of shoes until he was about twelve years old. Yet, by the time he was twenty-eight, he had beaten all-odds to become a succesful manager of a leading financial services company, rubbing shoulders with influential leaders and dining with business people.

This Mogusii lad whose parents once labored on the farm plucking tea for 500 shillings a month; This erstwhile boy who once wore badly patched pair of shorts and trekked 3 miles, barefoot, everyday to school went to be elected the youngest County Assembly Speaker. This polite chap who failed the first a dozen times he tried to debate in high-school is today one of the most eloquent, brash-breezy visionary leader in the country.

Moffat speaks in an intensively exuberant and conversational manner and has always said that the fear of God is the beggining of wisdom.

Education

Young Moffat had to struggle for an education, for hard luck was always battering away at the tea estates where his parents labored to earn a living. He stayed at the camp and commuted three miles barefoot to Mogusii Primary School each day. At home, he fetched water, cut the wood, and did his homework by the flickering light of an oil lamp until his eyes blurred and he began to nod.

After completing his primary, Teya joined Mwongori High School. He was twelve years then and although he was one of the isolated half-dozen who couldn't afford to board in school he was much happier to make daily commutes...

Work

After graduating from college, Moffat started selling insurance to individual and corporate clients in Nairobi. It was a painstakingly difficult assignment. In spite of all his boundless energy and enthusiasm, he was met by defeat after defeat. But he never gave up. Within two years, he had taken an unproductive territory that had stood in the last place and had boosted it to first place among all of the company's territories.
