WHY IS MOFFAT GOOD FOR NYAMIRA?

At this moment in the history of our county, Nyamira needs a leadership collective with integrity, commitment and capability.

Moffat Teya has all the attributes needed to confront the challenges facing the county.
HE IS TOUGH ON CORRUPTION

Moffat has a plan to end all forms of corruption. He has already declared that County Government must be strengthened by reducing wastage, appointing the right people, and ensuring more effective oversight, management and accountability. Under his leadership bribery, favouritism, nepotism, cronyism and embezzlement of funds will be a thing of the past.

HE IS COMPETENT AND CAPABLE

In every role he has undertaken, Teya has demonstrated exceptional skills and ability. He has been successful as a Manager, Strategist, County Assembly Speaker , family-man, business person and church elder. He has a good understanding of the economy, culture, law, governance and national politics.

HE IS A VISIONARY, A UNIFIER AND A BUILDER

Teya stands for the unity and equal opportunity for all the people of Nyamira. He is committed to the renewal and rebuilding of our county and a return to the values envisioned in the constitution.

HE IS COMMITTED TO TRANSFORMATION, TRANSPARENCY AND TRUTHFULNESS

He stands for transparent and effective governance that leads the people in the fundamental transformation of the economy and society.
